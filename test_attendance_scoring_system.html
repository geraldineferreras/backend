📊 Attendance Scoring System (6-Point Scale)

Present: 6 points (100%)
Late: 4 points (67%)
Excused: 5 points (83%)
Absent: 0 points (0%)

Formula: (Total Earned Score / (Total Sessions × 6)) × 100

Attendance Calculation Examples

Example 1: Student with 5 sessions

Attendance: 3 Present, 1 Late, 1 Absent

Present (3 × 6): 18 points
Late (1 × 4): 4 points
Absent (1 × 0): 0 points
Total Earned: 22 points
Max Possible (5 × 6): 30 points
Percentage: (22/30) × 100 = 73.33%
